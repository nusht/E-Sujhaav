Our Mission

Our mission with this project is to provide ​each and every student with the option to ​give suggestion or complaint without feeling ​hesitated or pressured by his peers. If the ​student is facing any kind of mental or ​physical abuse from any peer, teacher or ​family, we, the students of Government ​Model Senior Secondary-33D, strive to ​provide students with the 'anytime' access ​so the student's complaints are being paid ​attention to.

We are hoping to resolve your problem or ​look after your complaints as soon as ​possible. Our team desires to accomplish our ​mission of helping and listening students and ​letting them know that their Sujhav is being ​heard.

E-Sujhav is by the students, for the students.
